Engineering Zone Company Limited Formed and registered in 2017 at Dammam, K.S.A, sole owned company founded by Mr. Abdullah Al-Shamrani. The company provides leading Professionals industrial support services, industrial inspection as well as services for heavy and light equipment. The core business includes: lifting gear inspection, health and safety management, quality environment service, equipment’s calibration and certification, oil gas and water pipe line services and inspection, supplying of tools and machinery, etc. Engineering zone provides quality services to the various industrial fields.

Our business partner and sister company, Saleha Abdullah Al-Shamrani Est., which registered in 2015, is working complementarily with us. SALEHA has been engaged in different business activities and scopes include Rig move, earth moving equipment rental, and supplying of qualified riggers I, II, III, certified by Aramco which is a extremely limited resource in K.S.A.
